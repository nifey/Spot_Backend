Symptoms##Initially small dark spots form on older foliage near the ground.
Leaf spots are round, brown and can grow up to half inch in diameter.
Larger spots have target like concentric rings and tissue around spots often turns yellow.%%Biological control##Use pathogen-free seed, or collect seed only from disease-free plants.
