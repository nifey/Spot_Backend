Symptoms##1.Yellowing of the oldest leaves
2.In some cases, these leaves remain green. As the disease progresses, younger and younger leaves collapse.
3.Eventually, large portions of the tissues turn a brick red to brown color.%%Biological control##Sanitation by immediate removal of diseased plants with surrounding soil from the field.
During cultivation avoid root injury.%%Chemical control##Use of plant injection @ 2 per cent Carbendazim plus Agallol or Aretan soil drench is claimed as an effective method of freeing the planting stock of the pathogen and protection of new plants.
The additions of highly antagonistic organisms to soil around banana roots give protection to plants against wilt.
